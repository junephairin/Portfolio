During my freshman year of highschool, I took Progrmming I and Programming II. That was when I first discovered my love for coding. However, it didnt happen immediately. I remembered the feeling of anxiousness the second I submitted work to Mark, my progamming teacher. "What if i uploaded the file incorrectly?", "What if my page turned out to be ugly?", "What if my classmates laugh at my work?". The 14 year old me was very nervous about making mistakes and getting laughed at by my computer skills. But not long after, these kinds of thoughts turned into "I hope i did it correctly", "I can't wait until my classmates see it", and "I'm excited for tomorrow". Improvement huh? Little my little, I started to like programming even more. And now here i am. sitting behind my lenovo Yoga. making my own portfolio. below are the files that i have created, that made me who I am today. a crappy coder. no I'm just kidding. These files made me improve a LOT on my skills and gave me hope. I give you no guarantees that you will find it all pretty and remarkable, but.. Enjoy :)
